Location: UCSD

John S. •  4th year •  Employee at AS Design Studio

This is a project-based class, so it gives you something to put on your reusme. It also reinforces UX principles and gives you good practice with design applications. You learn the essentials like prototyping, storyboarding, needfinding, etc that are useful skills to have if you plan on pursuing a future in UX design. When I took this class, we also had to make a portfolio, which I appreciated, because I needed some encouragement to get mine done, and since it was an assignment, I had no choice but to make one.
